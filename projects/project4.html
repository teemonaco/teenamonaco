Challenges & Solutions

We faced several challenges such as addressing accessibility issues, fixing legacy code, and ensuring WCAG compliance. Solutions included improving color contrast and fixing user feedback loops on error messages.

Impact & Results

Post-redesign, we saw a 25% decrease in cart abandonment and improved usability across both mobile and desktop platforms.
