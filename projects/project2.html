Discover Screen

The Discover screen provides users with a personalized feed of articles and updates. The date filter feature allows users to view trending topics over time, helping them track important developments.

Favorites Screen

Users can save their favorite topics and articles to access later, keeping important resources readily available in their personalized stream.

Advanced Search and Filtering

The advanced search supports keyword combinations, Boolean operators, and category selections to ensure precise results. Users can further refine their search with relevance, date, and summary filters, allowing for an optimized search experience tailored to specific industry needs.

My Stream

Personalized content streams keep users updated on critical topics, with a clear, accessible layout for reviewing updates and saving articles for later.

Account Settings

The account settings screen allows users to adjust preferences, including notification settings, stream content, and general account configurations.
