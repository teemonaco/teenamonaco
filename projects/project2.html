User Personas

Stakeholder at Financial Company

John is a high-level financial analyst who uses InfoPro to stay updated on daily key articles his company pushes. He is always on the go, relying on mobile notifications to be well-prepared for meetings and decision-making.

Researcher at Pharma Company

Mary is a researcher struggling to find relevant information about drug phases and clinical trials. She uses InfoPro's advanced search and notifications to stay up-to-date on the latest regulatory news and trials while on the go.

Government Policy Advisor

David is a government advisor who tracks legislation and industry updates. InfoPro's preset streams and personalized notifications help him stay informed about real-time developments in the healthcare and life science industries.
